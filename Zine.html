Product Campaign

Brand: Kashi
Target Market:
Young adults who try to be healthy but don’t have a set routine or a lot of time.
Target Audience:
- Not enough time to eat a full breakfast
- Too lazy to meal prep, so they don’t have proper nutrition intakes
- Always on their way to their next destination
- Not a fan of the taste of heavy protein bars
Rationale:
Kashi is a brand that not only wants to create healthy options, but also make sure to satisfy your tastebuds. These granola bars are light to the stomach but also filled with healthy goodness. Even if you don’t have time to pre your own snacks, these can be brought with you anywhere! This is not a compromise but rather a choice for better, healthier snacking habits.

Role
Branding Identity, Photography
